The Perfect PB & J

Description

This peanut better and jelly sandwitch is my favorite sandwitch. it has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Any peanut butter and jam will work but my recomendation is to use grape jelly and all natural peanut butter

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of all natural peanut butter

Directions

  1. Place Peanut Butter on one slice of bread
  2. Place Jam on the other slice of bread
  3. Close it
  4. Toast it
